## Minimum proof required before any promotion

- [ ] Screenshots or logs show tool/plugin provenance, command output, background work, and parent-session state.
- [ ] At least one confusing or missing inspectability moment is captured, not smoothed over.
- [ ] The prototype checklist export is attached.
- [ ] Human reviewer notes distinguish direct observations from inferred trust.
- [ ] Rubric score and notes are filled before editing README/prototype/skill claims.

## Decision gate

Choose exactly one:

- [ ] **Promote to reusable skill** — evidence shows the checklist reliably catches actionable inspectability gaps and produces patchable recommendations.
- [ ] **Pilot-only** — useful for Hermes/Mission Control reviews, but not yet broad enough for a general agent-UX skill.
- [ ] **Iterate** — the structure helped, but fields, scoring, or handoff steps need revision before another review.
- [ ] **Hold** — evidence is incomplete, non-observational, or mostly template-level.
